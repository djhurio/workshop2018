University Dormitory No. 8

  • Twin rooms are available for single or double use.
  • Price per room, per night is 30.00 €. Payments can be made in cash or by card.
  • A bathroom and fridge is available in each room.
  • Internet is available for a small payment (0.61 € for seven days).
  • Kitchen in combination with lounge is available on each floor.
  • Dormitory is very close to the workshop venue (200 m).
  • Served breakfast is not available. Breakfast will be organised in the university cafeteria (the workshop venue) for additional price.
  • Reservation of rooms will be done by the workshop organisers.

Hotel "Jelgava"
