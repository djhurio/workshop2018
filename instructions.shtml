Instructions for Speakers

Lectures

It is possible to publish lecture material in the workshop proceedings. Lector can choose the form of publication. It can be an abstract, a paper or slides. Please send your lecture material to be included in the workshop proceedings till 17 July 2018 by e-mail to BNU2018@csb.gov.lv.

Contributed Papers

Participants presenting a contributed paper are kindly asked to submit a paper. A paper should be maximum 4 pages long. This is necessary because a discussant will be assigned to each contributed paper. A paper will help a discussant to prepare in advance for a discussion. The collection of contributed papers will be published by the organisers before the workshop. Please send your contributed paper to be included in the workshop proceedings till 17 July 2018 by e-mail to BNU2018@csb.gov.lv.

Templates for Abstracts and Papers

Please use provided templates for abstracts and papers to be published. There are templates for LaTeX and Microsoft Word:

  • LaTeX template (local files)
  • LaTeX template (ShareLaTeX online editor)
  • Microsoft Word template

Time

There will be 45 minutes for each lecture.

There will be 24 minutes for each contributed paper (exception is Friday with 22.5 minutes for a paper). You should limit your presentation to 15 minutes so to leave enough time for a discussion and questions from the audience.

Room

The main auditorium (212) of the workshop will be equipped with a computer, a projector, and a blackboard.
