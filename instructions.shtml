Instructions for Speakers

Lectures

Main and invited lecturers are invited to publish their material (abstract or paper) of the lectures in the workshop publication. This is not mandatory. Please send your abstract or paper if you wish it to be included in the workshop publication till 17 June 2018 by e-mail to BNU2018@csb.gov.lv.

Contributed Papers

Participants presenting a contributed paper are kindly asked to submit a paper. A paper should be maximum 4 pages long. This is necessary because a discussant will be assigned to each contributed paper. A paper will help a discussant to prepare in advance for a discussion. The collection of contributed papers will be published by the organisers before the workshop. Please send your contributed paper to be included in the workshop publication till 17 June 2018 by e-mail to BNU2018@csb.gov.lv.
