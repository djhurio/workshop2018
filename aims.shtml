Aims and Scope

The aim of the workshop is to maintain the cooperation between survey statisticians in Baltic and Nordic countries, Ukraine and Belarus. More information about the Baltic-Nordic-Ukrainian Network on Survey Statistics are available on BNU website.

The objectives are to present recent achievements and results, learn from teachers and colleagues, discuss the future, share opinions and experience and strengthen contacts between survey statisticians.
