Arrival

Most participants are expected to arrive to Jelgava through Rīga. It is possible to take a taxi from Rīga International Airport directly to Jelgava city. It will cost around 40 euros. Taxify is very convenient and reliable application for hiring a taxi: Android, iPhone.

You can also use public transport for the traveling. See the map for the directions.

Rīga International Airport -> Rīga centre

Rīga International Airport is only ten kilometres away from the Rīga city centre. A taxi ride from the airport to the central Rīga takes between ten and twenty minutes, depending on traffic, and costs approximately EUR 10–13. Taxify is very convenient and reliable application for hiring a taxi: Android, iPhone

It is also possible to go by bus no. 22 or express bus no. 322. It drives to and from the airport and departs every 10–20 minutes. There is a ticket vending machine right at the airport bus stop and the cost for one drive is EUR 1.15, it is also possible to buy a ticket for one drive from the bus driver for EUR 2.00. It takes around 26 minutes to get from the airport to the city centre. More information.

Rīga centre -> Rīga International Airport

The same bus no. 22 or express bus no. 322 can be used to get from Rīga centre to the Rīga International Airport. The stop named "13. janvāra iela" is convenient to go to the airport. There is a convenience mini-store "Narvesen" where it is possible to buy bus tickets for EUR 1.15. It takes around 32 minutes to get from the city centre to the airport.
