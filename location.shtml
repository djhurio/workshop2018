Location

Jelgava has been chosen as the host city of the workshop. Jelgava is a notable industrial, administrative, educational and culture centre of Zemgale region in Latvia. Jelgava is located just 42 km away from Riga.

Traveling between Riga and Jelgava can be made by train or bus. The most convenient way will be to use bus because the bus station in Jelgava is very close (400 m) to the workshop venue. It is also closer to the student hostel and hotels if compared to the train station. Buses are going on much more frequent schedule.

The bus schedules:

  • from Riga to Jelgava
  • from Jelgava to Riga

It takes approximately one hour to travel between Riga and Jelgava. The bus station is next to the train station in Riga. The bus stop to go to the Riga Airport is also close.

You can take taxi to travel between Jelgava and Riga or Riga International Airport (RIX). It will cost approximately 40 EUR.

The venue of the workshop will be the Faculty of Economics and Social Development (Latvia University of Life Sciences and Technologies).

Weather forecast for Jelgava is available at www.yr.no or www.gismeteo.com.
