Location

Valmiera has been chosen as the host city of the workshop. Valmiera is the cultural and administrative centre of Vidzeme region in Latvia. Valmiera is located around 110km from Riga.

The venue of the workshop will be the Vidzeme University of Applied Sciences.

Weather forecast for Valmiera is available at www.yr.no or www.gismeteo.com.
