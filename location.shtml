Location

Jelgava has been chosen as the host city of the workshop. Jelgava is a notable industrial, administrative, educational and culture centre of Zemgale region in Latvia. Jelgava is located just 42 km away from Riga.

The venue of the workshop will be the Faculty of Economics and Social Development (Latvia University of Agriculture).

Weather forecast for Jelgava is available at www.yr.no or www.gismeteo.com.
